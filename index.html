JEWELLERY INDUSTRY SERVICES & RESOURCES

THE BLUE BOOKS

The Blue Books are definitive sets of grading standards and nomenclature for diamonds, coloured gemstones, pearls, coral and precious metals, and gemmological laboratories. They can be downloaded at no cost.

THE RETAILER'S REFERENCE GUIDE

Available both online and as downloadable PDFs, the Retailer's Reference Guide is an indispensable information resource and sales tool for the staff of jewellery stores. It is being made available at no cost.

RESPONSIBLE SOURCING TOOLKIT

Enabling all members of the jewellery and gem sector to conduct responsible sourcing due diligence on their supply chains, the Responsible Sourcing Toolkit is comprised of digital files of can be downloaded at no cost and adapted by the user.

JEWELLERY INDUSTRY SUSTAINABILITY OFFICER PROGRAMME

The accredited Jewellery Industry Sustainability Officer Online Training & Certification Programme provides the knowledge necessary for applying practices related to Sustainability and CSR in jewellery businesses. Certification is recognised worldwide.

JEWELLERY INDUSTRY GREENHOUSE GAS MEASUREMENT INITIATIVE

The World Jewellery Confederation developed the Jewellery Industry Greenhouse Gas Measurement Initiative to help companies within the jewellery industry measure their environmental impact, reduce and offset it, and protect themselves and the industry as a whole.

CIBJO'S DO'S & DON'TS GUIDE

Available in 16 languages, CIBJO's Do's & Don'ts guide is a simplified aide to assist those selling diamonds, coloured gemstones, corals, pearls and/or any related artificial products understand the obligations they have towards their customers.
